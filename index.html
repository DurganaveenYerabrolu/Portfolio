EventSphere

Smart Event Management System is a web-based platform developed using Spring Boot, React, Java, and MySQL. It enables users to manage events with role-based access for Admins, Customers, and Workers. Customers can book events by selecting themes, cakes, food packages, and entertainment options. Admins handle booking approvals and assign workers based on their registered skills. Workers can manage their profiles and view assigned bookings. The system also features live event progress tracking and automated notifications to enhance coordination and ensure a smooth event experience.
